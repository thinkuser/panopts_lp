# Panoptes — Google Ads Keyword Research
**Date:** 2026-06-18  
**Platform:** Google Search  
**Geo:** Global English + Israel (parallel)  
**Budget:** $1,000–$3,000/mo  
**Goals:** Free trial signups (cold) + Demo bookings (warm/retargeting)  

> **Note:** This is an emerging/nascent category. Volume estimates are based on market reasoning, not live tool data. Validate and refine with Google Keyword Planner before launching. Many of these terms have low volume today but high growth trajectory.
